We are moving forward to our first page on the blog! This is the home page of our blog. It will display all of the posts we have written ordered by the date they were created and some more plugin components.

Actually, it will not display anything unless you pass it the plugins you want to render.

In The Bit blog, for example, we used the [featured posts](https://bit.dev/teambit/blog/plugins/featured-posts), [post tags](https://bit.dev/teambit/blog/plugins/post-tags), and the [newsletter](https://bit.dev/teambit/blog/plugins/newsletter) plugins.
